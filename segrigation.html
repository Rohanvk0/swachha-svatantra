Waste Segregation
Waste segregation is important because it helps in proper disposal, recycling, and reducing environmental pollution. It conserves resources, prevents health hazards, and makes waste management more efficient. The main types are *dry waste* (paper, plastic, metal), *wet waste* (food scraps, garden waste), *sanitary waste* (diapers, sanitary pads, medical waste), and *e-waste* (old electronics, batteries).
